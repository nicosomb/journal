[13 years ago (3 april 2013)](https://nicolas.loeuillet.org/billets/2013/04/03/poche-pour-remplacer-instapaper-pocket-et-readability/), between a bottle and a diaper, I started a little side project: "poche", an open source alternative to Pocket, a (closed and proprietary) read-it-later application. 

We've overcome quite a few obstacles. But today, Pocket (and others) is dead, and wallabag (the name since 2014) is still here 🎂

The wallabag ecosystem is very rich, wallabag is translated in dozens of languages, we have browsers addons, smartphones apps.  
I have a great team by my side to maintain all this ecosystem.  
I opened a SaaS based on wallabag on 2016. 

I'm so proud of this child 🦘 (I'm also proud of my real children, but it's not the topic 😅). 

See you in 13 years?
